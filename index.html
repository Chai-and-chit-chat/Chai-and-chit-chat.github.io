North Dallas

Chai and Chit Chat started in the wake of the pandemic. After months of being home alone, a group decided to get together for chai at a local restaurant. What came out of that was a desire to start a community where South Asian ladies help and support one another as they try to navigate life.

About us

About Chai and Chit Chat (C3)

Chai and Chit Chat (C3) is more than a community—it’s a movement of connection, celebration, and empowerment for Desi women in North Dallas and beyond. Founded in 2021 by the visionary Mercy Strickland, C3 began as a simple potluck gathering and has since blossomed into a thriving 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization with over 6,000 members and nearly 70 subgroups.

At C3, we believe in the power of shared experiences and collective growth. Guided by our motto—“Fostering Friendship, Building Community, and Uplifting Together”—we bring together women from diverse backgrounds to share their passions, navigate challenges, and celebrate life’s milestones.

What We Offer

C3 is a welcoming space where women can:

1. Explore interests through dedicated subgroups, including cooking, fitness, travel, crafts, social work, dance, and karaoke, etc.

2. Find support and solidarity in specialized groups for single moms, soul sisters, and job seekers.

3. Build lasting connections through vibrant events, activities, and meetups.

With no membership fees and a commitment to inclusivity, C3 ensures that every Desi woman has a place to belong and thrive.

Our Leadership

Mercy Strickland’s compassionate leadership has been the driving force behind C3’s success. Her journey, marked by a courageous move across the Atlantic, is a testament to her commitment to creating a safe haven for Desi women in a foreign land. Beyond her organizational efforts, Mercy supports women facing domestic challenges, offering them the resources and empowerment to reclaim their lives.

Our Vision

C3 is built on the belief that small, meaningful actions can create powerful transformations. We aim to foster a legacy of friendship, support, and empowerment for generations of desi women to come. At C3, we’re turning dreams into realities, challenges into triumphs, and connections into lifelong bonds.

Join us as we continue to grow, inspire, and uplift—together.

How to Join C3

Interested in becoming part of our vibrant community? Then be sure to attend our in-person Newcomer’s Meetup, held every Sunday and announced in the C3 Facebook group. This in-person session gives you a warm introduction to our mission, activities, and fellow members.

After attending the meetup, you’ll be added to our C3 WhatsApp Group. If you reside in a city with its own dedicated group, you’ll be added to that specific group as well. The main group acts as your gateway to all C3 subgroups, which cater to a variety of interests and community outreach initiatives.

Each main group and subgroup is led by an administrator who guides members and organizes activities. Once you’re part of the main group, you’re free to explore and join a limited number of subgroups that align with your interests. Additionally, the main group hosts monthly activities throughout the year, providing even more opportunities to connect, share, and grow within the vibrant C3 community.
